Explore Non-Fiction Books

Learn about the world and expand your knowledge.

Sapiens: A Brief History of Humankind

Yuval Noah Harari's "Sapiens" is a masterful exploration of the history of humankind. This book covers the history of humans from the Stone Age to the modern day.

Educated: A Memoir

Tara Westover's memoir "Educated" is a powerful story of survival, self-invention, and the pursuit of knowledge. It traces her journey from growing up in a strict family in rural Idaho to earning a PhD from Cambridge University.

Becoming

Michelle Obama's "Becoming" is an inspiring memoir that reveals the intimate journey of the former First Lady, from her childhood in Chicago to her years in the White House.

The Immortal Life of Henrietta Lacks

Rebecca Skloot's "The Immortal Life of Henrietta Lacks" tells the story of the woman whose cancer cells were used without her knowledge, contributing to many medical breakthroughs.
